PACTAC Video: Garnet Hertz

Professor Garnet Hertz, "Rethinking Technology: Art and Design"

We are very pleased to announce that the archived video from Professor Garnet Hertz's's recent PACTAC presentation, titled "Rethinking Technology: Art and Design," is now available at the PACTAC website:

http://pactac.net/2015/04/rethinking-technology-art-and-design/

In addition to the video of Professor Hertz's presentation, the audio version of the discussion period is also available.

Dr. Garnet Hertz is Canada Research Chair in Design and Media Arts and Associate Professor in the Faculty of Design and Dynamic Media at Emily Carr University.

Sponsored by the Pacific Centre for Technology and Culture (PACTAC) and the Maker Lab in the Humanities.
