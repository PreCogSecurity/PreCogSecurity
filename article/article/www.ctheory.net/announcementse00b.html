Critical Digital Studies (2nd Edition) and companion website update

Critical Digital Studies: A Reader (2nd edition)

Edited by Arthur and Marilouise Kroker
University of Toronto Press

The Critical Digital Studies companion website can be accessed at http://criticaldigitalstudies.net/.

From the editors of CTheory comes a ground-breaking guide to digital culture, written by theorists and artists who invented it.

Contributors include: Jackson 2bears, Michael Betancourt, William Bogard, Micha Cárdenas, Julie Clarke, Jordan Crandall, Mahesh Daas, Michael Dartnell, Francesca De Nicolò, Greig de Peuter, Sara Diamond, Steve Dixon, Nick Dyer-Witheford, Mark Featherstone, Alexander R. Lee, Lev Manovich, Christina McPhee, Paul D. Miller (DJ Spooky), Charles Tonderai Mudede, Samuel Nunn, Jussi Parikka, Stephen Pfohl, Tony D. Sampson, Gregory Sholette, Jaimie Smith-Windsor, Stelarc, Eugene Thacker, and James Tully
