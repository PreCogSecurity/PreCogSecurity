PACTAC Video: Frances Dyson

Frances Dyson -- Disaffective Voices and Posthuman Subjects

We are very pleased to announce that the archived video from Professor Frances Dyson's recent PACTAC presentation, titled "Disaffective Voices and Posthuman Subjects," is now available at the PACTAC website:

http://pactac.net/2014/12/disaffective-voices-and-posthuman-subjects/

In addition to the video of Professor Dyson's presentation, the audio version of the question period is also available.

Frances Dyson is Emeritus Professor of Cinema and Technocultural Studies at the University of California, Davis, and Visiting Professorial Fellow at the National Institute for Experimental Arts, University of New South Wales. She is the author of The Tone of Our Times: Sound, Sense, Economy, and Ecology (MIT Press, forthcoming October 2014) and Sounding New Media: Immersion and Embodiment in the Arts and Culture (University of California Press, 2009).
