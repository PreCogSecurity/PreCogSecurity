PACTAC Video: Nikolas Kompridis

Nikolas Kompridis -- The Right to the Human Condition: Arendt, Normativity, and Utopian Politics

We are very pleased to announce that the archived video from Professor Nikolas Kompridis's recent PACTAC presentation titled "The Right to the Human Condition: Arendt, Normativity, and Utopian Politics" is now available at the PACTAC website:

http://pactac.net/2014/06/the-right-to-the-human-condition-arendt-normativity-and-utopian-politics/

Nikolas Kompridis is a Research Professor in Philosophy and Political Thought and the Director of the Institute for Social Justice at the Australian Catholic University in Sydney. He is the author of The Aesthetic Turn in Political Thought (Bloomsbury, 2014), Critique and Disclosure: Critical Theory between Past and Future (MIT, 2006), and Philosophical Romanticism (Routledge, 2006), as well as over fifty articles on a broad spectrum of topics in philosophy and political theory.
