SPECIAL ANNOUNCEMENT: BLUESHIFT SERIES

CTHEORY is very pleased to announce the BLUESHIFT Series.

Technology has suddenly blueshifted, moving closer and closer to the epicenter of society, politics, and culture. Already visible in the cerulean sky of data are new constellations of the future, waiting to be interpreted for their political implications and social entanglements by writers and artists involved in the BLUESHIFT Series.
